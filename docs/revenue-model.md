# MODUS Revenue Model

> v9.0.0 Anima — February 2026

## Executive Summary

MODUS is a B2C AI-powered universe simulation platform where users create worlds populated by autonomous, philosophically-grounded agents. Revenue comes from tiered subscriptions, a creator marketplace, and education licensing. Unlike B2B competitors (Simule AI at $100M valuation), MODUS targets individual creators, educators, and hobbyists with an open-source core and premium cloud features.

---

## Pricing Tiers

### 🆓 Free — $0/mo
- 1 world, 10 agents
- Local LLM only (Ollama)
- Community templates
- Basic analytics (Observatory)

### ⚡ Pro — $9/mo
- Unlimited worlds, 50 agents per world
- Cloud LLM access (Gemini, GPT-4o)
- Priority simulation speed
- Advanced analytics & export
- Custom world templates

### 🎬 Studio — $29/mo
- Everything in Pro
- 200 agents per world
- REST API access
- White-label embedding
- Marketplace publishing
- Priority support

### 🎓 Education — $4/student/mo
- Classroom management dashboard
- Curriculum-aligned scenarios (ethics, sociology, economics)
- Student progress tracking
- Shared world collaboration (up to 30 students)
- Teacher god-mode controls

### 🏢 Enterprise — Custom
- Dedicated infrastructure
- Custom agent behaviors & models
- SSO/SAML integration
- SLA guarantees
- On-premise deployment option

## Marketplace

Creator-driven template marketplace with **70/30 revenue split** (creator/MODUS):

- **World Templates**: Pre-built scenarios (Medieval Village, Space Colony, Dystopia)
- **Agent Packs**: Personality archetypes, behavior trees, profession sets
- **Visual Themes**: Tile sets, UI skins, sound packs
- **Curriculum Modules**: Education-specific scenario bundles

Estimated marketplace GMV: $50K (Y1) → $500K (Y2) → $2M (Y3)

### MODUS Differentiators
1. **Spinoza Philosophy**: Agents driven by conatus (self-preservation drive), affects, and ethical reasoning — not just utility maximization
2. **Open-Source Core**: Community trust, extensibility, transparency
3. **Creator-First**: Marketplace empowers users to monetize their creativity
4. **Local-First**: Full functionality with Ollama, no cloud dependency
5. **B2C Focus**: Accessible to individuals, not just enterprises

## Go-to-Market Strategy

### Phase 1: Community Building (Q1-Q2 2026)
- Open-source launch on GitHub
- Dev.to / Hacker News / Reddit campaigns
- YouTube tutorial series ("Build Your First AI World")
- Discord community with early adopter rewards

### Phase 2: Creator Economy (Q3 2026)
- Marketplace launch
- Template creation contests ($5K prize pool)
- Influencer partnerships (AI/gaming YouTubers)
- Product Hunt launch

### Phase 3: Education Push (Q4 2026)
- Pilot programs with 10 schools/universities
- Conference presence (ISTE, BETT)
- Curriculum partnerships
- Case studies and whitepapers

### Phase 4: Scale (2027)
- Enterprise sales team
- Localization (10+ languages)
- Mobile companion app
- API ecosystem expansion
